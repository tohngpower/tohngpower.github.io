Frequently Asked Questions

A useful FAQ section.

How do I get API key for free?

You can follow instruction in pdf file. You have free trial of API Key 3 months. Or apply new account after 3 months to get another 3 months.

How much does it cost if I want to continue using after free trial?

Normally 1 word = 1 token. Cost for GPT3.5 turbo is about $1.50/1M tokens. You can update latest price from OpenAI website

How do I go to database or other menu?

You can press MIC button to speak "database" to open database. Or type "database" and click send button. Or long click at Wandee image to open menu.

AI cannot speak Japanese.

You can click "setting button" and change langauge to Japanese.

How to create an image?

You can type "imageGEN" and follow with your prompt for image details.

How do we know how many tokens using now?

You can check by type "check usage" or press MIC to speak "check usage". But it maynot accurate. Or you can check directly from OpenAI platform website for accurate usage./
